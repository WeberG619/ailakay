The story

Sa k pase

A few weeks ago, I installed ChatGPT on my sister's phone and showed her she could speak to it in Creole. Then I showed her she could point her camera at anything — a form, an app, a piece of mail — and it would explain what she was looking at, in Creole.

She signed up for the paid version in five minutes.

I'm building AI Lakay so every Haitian Creole speaker who wants to use AI can have that same five minutes. No English required. No tech background required. Just your phone, your language, and someone showing you what's possible.
